Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate Chip Cookies from Pailin:

Deliciously sweet and crispy chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients (yields about 36 cookies)

  • 285 g unsalted butter, room temp (about 2 sticks) OR 214 g vegetable oil (about a cup)
  • 230 g granulated sugar (about a cup)
  • 285 g dark brown sugar (about 1.33 cups)
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs, room temp
  • 470 g all-purpose flour (about 3 cups)
  • 1 1/2 tsp fine salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 450g (1lb) dark chocolate discs/wafers
  1. Prep ingredients so they are room temp (eggs, butter)
  2. Place flour (470g) and salt (1.5 tsp) in a large mixing bowl. Sift baking powder (1.5 tsp) and baking soda (1.25 tsp) through a small sieve into the flour.
  3. Whisk everything together until well mixed.
  4. Soften room temp butter (285g) or vegetable oil (214g). Add brown (285g) and white (230g) sugar and mix on medium speed until the colour islightened and the mixture looks fluffy.
  5. Add vanilla (2 tsp) and room temp eggs (2), then until just well incorporated, scraping down sides as needed.
  6. Add dry ingredients in 4 installments, mixing just for a few seconds each time until the flour is no longer sitting on top of the dough. Use a wooden spoon to stir everything together.
  7. Add the chocolate discs then continue to mix on low speed just until all the flour is incorporated into the dough and the chocolate is evenly distributed.
  8. Portion the dough out into 2-inch balls and place them onto a tray or a big container. This is about 1.22 oz.
  9. Cover the dough tightly and refrigerate for 48 hours.
  10. When ready to bake, preheat oven to 350°F. Let it preheat for at least 30 minutes.
  11. Once the oven is ready, pull cookies out and put them on baking sheets lined with parchment paper or silicone mat. Make sure the cookies are at least 2 inches apart (you should only put 6 cookies on a half sheet pan).
  12. Bake only 1 cookie to test out.
  13. Bake in the middle rack for 15 mins until the edges are golden brown but the centre is still soft. **If after your first batch you find that the cookies are spreading too much, increase the temp to 375°F and bake for 11-12 minutes.
