Korean Chicken

Korean Chicken from Maangchi:

Sweet, sour, and spicy korean fried chicken coated in a delicious red sauce

Chicken and Dredge

  • 16 oz of chicken breast
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 large eggs

Sauce Ingredients

  • 4 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup honey/maple syrup
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup gochujang (SUBSTITUTE BELOW)
  • 2 teaspoons white vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons rice vinegar

Other Ingredients (Pan)

  • 2.5 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced

Gochujang Substitute (1/3 cup):

  • 1 tablespoons gochugaru
  • 0.5 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 0.5 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 0.5 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 0.5 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 0.5 teaspoon syrup (optional, to enhance sweetness if needed)
  1. Cook rice 30 min prior to serving, keep warm.
  2. Make sauce
  3. Start making chicken dredge
  4. Heat 2 inches of oil until the oil temperature reaches 330-350ºF. While waiting, make sauce and bread chicken.
  5. Once oil reaches temp and chicken is coated, add chicken to oil in 3-6 batches. Cook for 4-6 minutes, stirring occasionally until golden brown and slightly crunchy. Then strain chicken's oil in strainer.
  6. Make the sauce. Add vegetable oil and garlic cloves, then when fragrant, add sauce and stir until bubbling. Remove from heat.
  7. Heat oil until 350ºF, fry chicken for 3-6 minutes until very crispy. Make sure to TEMP chicken for 160 degrees
  8. Reheat sauce until bubbling, then stir in chicken.
  9. Scoop rice, plate chicken with green onions.
